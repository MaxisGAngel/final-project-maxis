Events and Activities

At Te Pā Tauira they offer a wide variety of events and activities across the year, this include trips to see glow worms, cultural nights, formal dinners, cooking classes and much more. I could keep going on about the events provided, but nstead I have derived a short list.

Stress Free Tuesday

Clay Making

Every Tuesday the hall hosts a 'Stress Free Tuesday' activty which typically consists of simple arts and crafts tasks.

Physical Activities

Just Dance

The hall has a wde vareity of physical activities ranging from sporting events, gym days, going to Leap or Just Dance. The good variety in these, allow for a wider range in personalities to join in on these physcial activties.

Thursday Nights

Pancakes

Every Thursday night during term time, the Grace Bible Chruch come to the hall to serve us pancakes. They are always happy for a chat and enjoy the students and the hall.
